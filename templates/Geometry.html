MATHEMATCS AND GOEMETRY

Theory

The Golden Ratio has unique mathematical properties. It is the only positive number whose square is one greater than itself. It is the only positive number whose reciprocal is one less than itself. It is also the found in limits and the Fibonacci series. These properties make it the unique solution to optimize design, in practicality and in beauty.

Φ, Φ, 1.618…, has two properties that make it unique among all numbers.

• If you square Φ , you get a number exactly 1 greater than itself: 2.618…, or

Φ² = Φ + 1.

• If you divide Φ into 1 to get its reciprocal, you get a number exactly 1 less than itself: 0.618…, or

1 / Φ = Φ – 1.

Φ has a unique additive relationship.

The powers of Φ have unusual properties in that they are related not only exponentially, but are additive as well. We know that

Φ²= Φ + 1

Which is the same as:

Φ² =Φ^1 + Φ^0

And this leads to the fact that for any n:

Φ^(n+2) = Φ^(n+1) + Φ^(n)

Thus each two successive powers of Φ add to the next one!
